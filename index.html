Hi! I am Roy

I'm currently an M.S. student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Tsing Hua University. My research interests include Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Speech, and Deep Knowledge Tracing. I work with Prof. Min Sun as a member of the Vision Science Lab on Deep Learning and its applications in Computer Vision. Recently, I focus on 3D Detection and Tracking in a monocular approach and also pixel-level representation learning.

When I was a B.S. student at the Department of Electrical Engineering, National Tsing Hua University. I used to do the research about Natural Language Processing and also be advised by Prof. Min Sun. From July 2019 to June 2020, I worked as a research intern in Research Center for Information Technology Innovation, Academia Sinica and study on Speech, including Speech Enhencement, Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) and Multi-speaker ASR.

Besides doing research, I also got an internship in Umbo Computer Vision Inc., as a Quality Assurance Intern from September 2019 to December 2019.
